Do you or your employer have an Ofsted unique reference number (URN)?

You can use the public Ofsted register (opens in a new tab) to look up a unique reference number (URN).

If you are a nanny or a childminder
If you are a nanny or a childminder you may not be listed on the public Ofsted register. You will need to check your email or registration certificate to find your URN.
If your employer is registered with Ofsted or you are registered with a childminder agency (CMA), you can use their URN.

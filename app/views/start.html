This service is for teachers who want to register:

  • to study for an NPQ
  • for the Additional Support Offer (ASO) for NPQ Headship

Before you start

You must:

  • only use this service if you are a teacher working in England, Guernsey, Jersey or the Isle of Man – if you are a teacher working outside these locations contact your provider directly to register
  • have your teacher reference number (TRN)
  • have your funding source confirmed – either a scholarship, school, college or personal funding
  • know which NPQ course you want to study
  • have chosen a provider

The Additional Support Offer is a targeted support package for new headteachers.

Who is this for?

You can register for the Additonal Support Offer if you:

  • have completed an NPQH
  • are studying for an NPQH
  • are about to start an NPQH

How is this paid for?

You may be eligible for DfE scholarship funding if you are a headteacher in your first 2 years of headship.

Or you can pay the cost of your Additional Support Offer another way.

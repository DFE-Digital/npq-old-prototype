Available courses

From September 2022, the following NPQ courses will be available:

  • NPQ Senior Leadership
  • NPQ Executive Leadership
  • NPQ Headship
  • NPQ Leading Teaching Development
  • NPQ Leading Teaching
  • NPQ Leading Behaviour and Culture
  • NPQ Early Years Leadership
  • NPQ Leading Literacy

Additional Support Offer for new headteachers

From autumn 2021 the additional support offer is a new targeted support package for teachers new to the role of headship.

It is for new headteachers in their first 2 years of a headship role who have either completed an NPQH or are currently taking an NPQH.

Providers

There are ten training providers across England which run NPQ training courses.

They are:

  • Ambition Institute
  • Best Practice Network (home of Outstanding Leaders Partnership)
  • Church of England
  • Education Development Trust
  • Institute of Teaching
  • Leadership Learning South East (LLSE)
  • School-Led Network
  • Teacher Development Trust
  • Teach First
  • UCL Institute of Education

Some providers may have specific criteria depending on your circumstances.

Check with your current employer to see if they recommended accredited partners for NPQ training.

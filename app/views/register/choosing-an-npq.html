Providers

There are 10 training providers running NPQ courses across England:

  • Ambition Institute
  • Best Practice Network (home of Outstanding Leaders Partnership)
  • Church of England
  • Education Development Trust
  • LLSE
  • National Institute of Teaching
  • School-Led Network
  • Teacher Development Trust
  • Teach First
  • UCL Institute of Education

Some providers may offer different NPQs and have specific criteria depending on your circumstances.

Check with your employer to see if they have recommended accredited partners for NPQ training.

If you are unsure whether you have updated your name on Teaching Regulation Agency records, you can request a reminder.

Request a TRN reminder by email

Send an email to qts.enquiries@education.gov.uk which includes your:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number

Request a TRN reminder by phone

Call the Teaching Regulation Agency on 020 7593 5394 to obtain your TRN.

Make sure you have your National Insurance number ready before you call.

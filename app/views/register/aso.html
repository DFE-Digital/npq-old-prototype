The Early Headship Coaching Offer is a package of structured face-to-face support for new headteachers.

Who is this for?

You can register for the Early Headship Coaching Offer if you:

  • have completed an NPQH
  • are studying for an NPQH
  • are about to start an NPQH

How is this paid for?

You may be eligible for DfE scholarship funding if you are a headteacher in your first 5 years of headship.

Or you can pay the cost of your Early Headship Coaching Offer another way.
